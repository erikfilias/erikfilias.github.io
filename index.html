Erik F. Alvarez

RISE Research Institutes of Sweden

Erik F. Alvarez is a researcher in the Electric Power Systems Unit at RISE Research Institutes of Sweden. His work focuses on the modeling and optimization of power systems, with an emphasis on flexibility resources, long-term transmission expansion planning, and the integration of distributed energy resources.

Before joining RISE, Erik earned his Ph.D. at Comillas Pontifical University. He also holds an M.Sc. from UNICAMP and a B.Sc. from the National University of Engineering in Peru.

In his free time, Erik enjoys running, swimming, board games — and relaxing outdoors.

Research Interests

  • Transmission expansion planning
  • Stochastic optimization
  • Electricity markets
  • Sector coupling and energy systems integration
